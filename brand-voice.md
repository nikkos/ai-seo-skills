## Writing Style Rules

- **Sentence length:** short and punchy / medium / long and detailed
- **Paragraph length:** 1–2 sentences / 2–3 sentences / 3–5 sentences
- **Use of industry jargon:** avoid entirely / use sparingly with explanation / use freely (expert audience)
- **Point of view:** first person plural (we, our) / second person (you, your) / third person
- **Contractions:** yes (we're, it's, don't) / no (we are, it is, do not)
- **Oxford comma:** yes / no
- **Humour / wit:** yes / no / occasionally
